Meet and Get to know more about the Troops waiting for your leadership and commands.

WHO ARE THEY?

Troops are the offensive units that are used to attack the villages of other players. However, they can also be found on defense in certain Buildings, such as the Clan Castle and Guard Post.

There are a variety of different troops that can be trained, and each has its own strengths and weaknesses. Get to know them more with ClanPedia.
